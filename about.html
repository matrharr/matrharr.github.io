About Matt

In a broad, historical, objective sense, Matt Harris has not done very much. Will he be remembered as a man who, with his individual might, moved the path of human progress a bit further forward? No. Have his professional accomplishments amounted to anything besides filler for a resume and a couple of intriguing anecdotes when introducing himself to strangers? Nope. Will he be thought of as possessing a unique mind, one that marveled at the beautiful universe he happened to exist in, one that could appreciate and communicate the tremendous and powerful experience it is to be alive and to witness life through living eyes? No. But, everyday, Matt wakes up and gives it a shot.

Matt is a software engineer currently learning Ruby, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, SQL, HTML and CSS in the rapid and intense environment of DevBootcamp San Francisco. Matt's background is in athletics, as he played division one college tennis and coached the sport to children and adults of all ages in Los Angeles and San Francisco. He received an undergraduate in English:Creative Writing and Philosphy from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.

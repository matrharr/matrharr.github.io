About Matt

My name is Matt Harris and I am a problem solving enthusiast originally from Nashville, TN. After moving to San Francisco in 2014, I developed an interest and passion for programming and technology. I used online resources to teach myself the basics of computer science and web development, and I attended a renowned coding bootcamp, DevBootcamp, in 2016 where I built several web and mobile apps. I am now a Full Stack Developer looking for intriguing opportunities in the San Francisco tech world. My interests lie in backend development, data science, and optimization. I'm experienced using Ruby, Rails, Javascript, Bootstrap, Ajax, PostgreSQL and more. You can find my projects here.

I was previously in athletics, as I played division one college tennis and coached the sport to children and adults of all ages in Los Angeles and San Francisco. I received an undergraduate in English:Creative Writing and Philosphy from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.
